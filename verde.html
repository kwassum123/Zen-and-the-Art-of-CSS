On Verde Moderna by Dave Shea

My Thoughts

This page out of the pushed examples is, in my humble opinion, the cleanest and most representative of zen out of the rest. In a past life, I studied Buddist philosophy and spirituality extensively. I became an expert on meditation and was able to focus all of my energy on my inner self. When I was first able to do so, I had an epiphany. I had achieved enlightenment. In my minds eye, all I was able to see was this most serene webpage. My consciousness was eternally wedded with that of Dave Shea's. I saw the beauty of simplicity and layout most easy to read. The implementation of natural themes and a soft green and beige color scheme are but visual icing on the page's cake.
